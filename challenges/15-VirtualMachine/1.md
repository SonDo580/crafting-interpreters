# Challenge 15.1

What bytecode instruction sequences would you generate for the following expressions:

```
1 * 2 + 3
1 + 2 * 3
3 - 2 - 1
1 + 2 * 3 - 4 / -5
```

(Remember that Lox does not have a syntax for negative number literals, so the -5 is negating the number 5.)

## Answer

```
. 1 * 2 + 3
OP_CONSTANT 0 (1)
OP_CONSTANT 1 (2)
OP_MULTIPLY
OP_CONSTANT 2 (3)
OP_ADD

. 1 + 2 * 3
OP_CONSTANT 0 (1)
OP_CONSTANT 1 (2)
OP_CONSTANT 2 (3)
OP_MULTIPLY
OP_ADD

. 3 - 2 - 1
OP_CONSTANT 0 (3)
OP_CONSTANT 1 (2)
OP_SUBTRACT
OP_CONSTANT 2 (1)
OP_SUBTRACT

. 1 + 2 * 3 - 4 / -5
OP_CONSTANT 0 (1)
OP_CONSTANT 1 (2)
OP_CONSTANT 2 (3)
OP_MULTIPLY
OP_ADD
OP_CONSTANT 3 (4)
OP_CONSTANT 4 (5)
OP_NEGATE
OP_DIVIDE
OP_SUBTRACT
```
